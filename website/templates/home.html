Instructions on Using the Population Flow Map

Info

The map utilizes a spatial sankey to show a flow of people from state to state. The visual of migration will show when you hover over a state's circle. The amount of people migrating will also show up under your cursor.

There are two (2) dropdowns. One that allow you to change the year of the data, and another to change the direction. The years of our data only includes 2015, 2016, and 2017. The direction can be migration into each state or migration out of each state.
